Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Who we are

This policy applies to affiliates.onlymatt.ca, operated by onlymatt. It explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data we collect

  • Twitter / X username — collected temporarily during the OAuth flow solely to verify your identity against your collaborator profile. It is not stored after authentication.
  • Collaborator profile data — name, public social link, booking information, and private contact details (email, contact handle) that you or the operator enter into the application. Stored in a private database (Turso / libSQL).
  • Session data — a short-lived browser session (sessionStorage) records which cards you have unlocked during your current visit. This data never leaves your browser.

3. How we use your data

  • To verify your identity when unlocking your private collaborator card.
  • To display your booking and contact information to the operator only.
  • Private email addresses are never returned in public API responses.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Twitter / X — to perform the OAuth 2.0 identity check. Governed by X's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosting provider. May process request logs in accordance with Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Turso — database provider. Data is stored encrypted at rest.

5. Data retention

Collaborator profile data is retained until explicitly deleted by the operator. Authentication tokens used during the OAuth flow expire within 10 minutes and are discarded immediately after use.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting the operator.

7. Cookies

During the Twitter OAuth flow we set three short-lived HttpOnly cookies (tw_cv, tw_state, tw_collab) to secure the authentication process. They expire in 10 minutes and are deleted as soon as the flow completes. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.

9. Contact

Questions about your data? Reach out via @onlymatt43 on X.
